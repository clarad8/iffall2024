ART337I - Communication Design: Interaction Foundations

Recipe Page

A website describing a recipe for creating fluffy banana pancakes. This recipe is super simple and comes together in just a few minutes. You don’t need any fancy ingredients or gadgets. These pancakes freeze well, so they’re perfect for meal prep. Make a batch on the weekend and enjoy them all week long!

Microsite - Creative Director

This Graffiti microsite is developed by Gioia Wang as the designer, and I was the creative director, giving the initial ideas and design suggestions along the way. The website is designed to showcase iconic graffiti art across New York City, exploring the history and evolution of graffiti culture from its rise to its decline and the forces that have shaped it over time.

Microsite - Designer

This microsite about Louis Kahn is developed by me with Anaeld Ramos as the creative director. This website presents a biography of Louis Kahn in a visual style that pays homage to his architectural designs. I was tasked with designing this website with a mainly charcoal gray themed background and hints of red and yellow throughout the pages.
